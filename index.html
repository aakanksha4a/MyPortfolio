About Me

Hello! I’m Aakanksha Solanki, a passionate Frontend Designer with a knack for crafting beautiful and user-friendly web pages. With a solid foundation in web design from my internship at Indore Municipal Corporation, I’ve honed my skills in creating engaging and innovative designs that enhance user experience. My journey in frontend design began with a fascination for how design and technology intersect to create seamless digital experiences. At Indore Municipal Corporation, I gained valuable hands-on experience working on a variety of web design projects, where I learned to blend functionality with aesthetics to meet both user needs and business goals. I thrive on experimenting with new design trends and techniques, and I’m always eager to tackle new challenges that push the boundaries of conventional web design. Whether it’s creating responsive layouts, optimizing user interfaces, or implementing modern design principles, I approach each project with creativity and attention to detail. When I’m not designing, I enjoy exploring new design tools and staying updated with the latest industry trends. I’m also a keen advocate of continuous learning and love to collaborate with others to bring fresh ideas to life. Feel free to explore my portfolio to see some of my recent work.
